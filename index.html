Sivulingqondo Means "Opening the Mind"

We believe that education, care, and nutrition form the foundation of strong communities. Through our afterschool programs, youth mentorship, and community kitchen, we support families in need and help the next generation grow with dignity and opportunity.

Our programs include academic intervention for grades R-7, skills development workshops, parent support groups, and nutrition initiatives that empower the entire community.
